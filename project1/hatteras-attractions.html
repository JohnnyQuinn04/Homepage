Attractions in Hatteras

Explore the stunning attractions that make Hatteras a unique destination.

National Seashore

The Cape Hatteras National Seashore offers breathtaking landscapes, sandy beaches, and a variety of outdoor activities, including fishing, surfing, and wildlife viewing.

UFO

The Frisco UFO is a quirky roadside attraction located in Frisco, North Carolina, featuring a flying saucer-shaped house originally built in the 1970s. Known for its retro-futuristic design, it draws curious tourists eager to see this unique piece of architecture, now part of the Outer Banks' offbeat charm.
