Historic Lighthouses

Explore the iconic lighthouses of the Outer Banks!

Cape Hatteras Lighthouse

The Cape Hatteras Lighthouse is not only the tallest lighthouse in the United States but also one of the most photographed. Climb the 257 steps for an incredible view!

Ocracoke Lighthouse

Ocracoke Lighthouse is the oldest lighthouse still in operation in North Carolina, guiding mariners since 1823. Its unique architecture is a favorite among visitors.

Bodie Island Lighthouse

Bodie Island Lighthouse is known for its stunning black-and-white stripes and has a rich history. Learn more about its preservation and role in maritime navigation.
